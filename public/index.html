Welcome to Shape Comparisons. Thank you for joining us today.

For this activity you will be asked to compare the size of two black boxes - a static one at the top of the screen and a changing one at the bottom of the screen. Here's how this works:

  • Click the small black circle at the botton of the screen to start
  • If the box at the bottom of the screen is larger than the one at the top, then click the "larger" button, and if it is smaller, click the "smaller" button
  • To get your next box, click the small black circle that appears at the bottom of the screen
  • Keep going with your comparisons until the activity concludes
Please do this as quickly and accurately as possible.

We will start you off with a practice round where you can try this out. The entire activity will take about 15 minutes.
